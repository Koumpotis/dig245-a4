Passage from my essay "My purpose":
I want to constantly evolve myself, through my decision making. The very same decision making that makes me feel insecure is the key for me to grow, is the only way for me to come as close as possible to my potential. We have convinced ourselves that we need to have a purpose, and somewhere along the way, we forgot we need to have ourselves.
The reason I am drawn to philosophy is because it seeks the truth, without imposing the definition you conclude to. Philosophy is the adult version of a playground.
