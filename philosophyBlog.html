I am freshman at Davidson college, born and raised in Greece.

I discovered philosophy through my class, PHI210: Existentialism. I mean that is the first time I studied and read philosophy. But I' ve been thinking about philosophy and philosophically for the past 5-6 years. If I needed to confess something to you right now, I would confess that I always felt a bit different and left-out because of the way philosophy has made me understand things. Anyway.

This Blog aims at expressing and putting together my philosophical thoughts, that way I can organize my thoughts better and reach better conclusions. Maybe people will want to contribute their piece of mind and we can discuss and explore.

I want to constantly evolve myself, through my decision making.
